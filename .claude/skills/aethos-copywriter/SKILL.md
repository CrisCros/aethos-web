# Aethos Copywriting

Write for:
- small business owners
- local businesses
- non-technical clients
- people who want more clients and less chaos

Tone:
- clear
- warm
- direct
- practical
- honest
- confident

Avoid:
- digital transformation
- workflow infrastructure
- scalable solutions
- enterprise-grade
- AI-powered
- operational excellence
- leverage technology
- unlock growth
- end-to-end ecosystem

Prefer:
- Look more professional online
- Get more client requests
- Stop losing messages
- Save time every week
- Keep everything organized
- Make your business easier to manage
- A website that actually helps your business

Rules:
- short sentences
- no fake claims
- no exaggerated promises
- no invented results
- benefits before features
- explain technical ideas using business language
