# Conversion Review

The website must answer quickly:
1. What do we do?
2. Who do we help?
3. What problem do we solve?
4. What should the visitor do next?

Review:
- hero clarity
- CTA visibility
- CTA wording
- service explanation
- contact flow
- trust signals
- page hierarchy
- friction before contact
- mobile conversion
- form simplicity

Avoid:
- too many CTAs
- vague CTAs
- long forms
- unclear service descriptions
- technical language before business value
- hiding the contact button
- overexplaining before asking for action

Preferred CTAs:
- Tell us about your business
- Start a project
- Let’s see if we can help
- Book a quick call
- Get a simple plan

Goal:
Make it easy for a small business owner to contact Aethos without pressure.
